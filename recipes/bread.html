Vegan Bread Pudding

Vegan bread pudding is easy and simple to make, and you probably have all or most of the ingredients in your cupboard or refrigerator already.

Ingredients
  • 4 cups cubed day-old bread
  • 3 cups soy milk
  • 1 cup raisins
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup chopped almonds (Optional)
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place bread cubes in a 9-inch baking pan. Stir soy milk, raisins, sugar, almonds, vanilla extract, and cinnamon together in a separate bowl and pour over the bread. Mix to combine.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown and heated through, about 30 minutes.
